FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and process.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A custom home typically takes 12-18 months from design to completion.

Do you work with clients who have their own architect?

Absolutely. We regularly collaborate with external architects and designers.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Abuja and its environs, including Asokoro, Maitama, Wuse, Garki, and surrounding areas.

How do you handle budgets and cost overruns?

We provide detailed cost estimates before construction begins and maintain strict budget tracking.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes. All our work comes with a comprehensive warranty. Structure work is warranted for 5 years.
